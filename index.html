THE IDEA

Simple, regular, and actually useful.

SuperGrowth is how we do growth at Supermetrics now. Once a month, you take a moment to reflect on what you've done and where you want to go — then talk it through with your manager. No big annual surprise. Just small, regular check-ins that build up over time.

WHAT HAPPENS & WHEN

Here's what happens and when.

NOW
📬

Week of April 20th: Your first prompt arrives.

Kabuto pings you in Slack with four short questions. Fill them in — takes about 10 minutes. You can also do it on the web if you prefer.

💬

Last two weeks of April: Your GROW chat.

Use one of your regular 1:1s to have a proper conversation with your manager about where you're going. They've already read your Growth Portfolio, so you can skip the catch-up and get to the good stuff.

🧠

First two weeks of May: Talent Talks.

Your manager and People Team sit down together. They talk about how the team is doing, who could use more support, and who might be ready for something new.

EVERY MONTH
🔁

Then it repeats.

Same rhythm, every month. It gets quicker and more useful each time.

🚀 Starting the week of April 20th — watch your Slack. Kabuto will ping you. The whole thing takes about 10 minutes.
YOUR GUIDE

Meet Kabuto.

Once a month, Kabuto shows up in your Slack. It asks four questions. You answer.

Then Kabuto does its thing — it takes your answers and carries them all the way to the Growth Portfolio dashboard, where your manager can read them before your next 1:1. Neither of you has to scramble to remember what happened last month.
